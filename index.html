Working With Me
Heather Potter

"My purpose is to bring people together to build and lead something meaningful that creates security and connection for myself and the people I care about."

What I Value
My Values
Security

Creating stability, opportunity, and protection for the people around me.

Wholeheartedness

Leading with care, honesty, and alignment even when it is uncomfortable.

Success

Building something meaningful, high-performing, and impactful.

Where I Shine
My Strengths
  • I am authentically me. What you see is what you get, no performance
  • I take accountability for my actions, including when I get it wrong
  • I can read a room and know when to lean in and when to hold back
  • I am curious about myself, other people, and what is actually going on beneath the surface
  • I stay grounded when things get hard. I don't spiral and I don't catastrophize
  • I address issues head-on, make decisions, and live with the results right or wrong
  • I genuinely care about people. Not as a management tactic, just because I do
  • I get things done and I try not to take any of it too seriously
Enneagram SP-3 · Competitive Achiever
Where I Struggle
My Improvers
  • I am working on being more strategic and thinking longer-term instead of moving straight to execution
  • I don't always know when to cut losses. I can sometimes keep pouring into something or someone too long
  • I delegate best when I have seen someone earn it. Getting there can take time, and I know I sometimes hold on longer than I need to while trust is being built
  • I sometimes soften or delay hard conversations when I am trying to protect a relationship
  • I have a tendency to become a workaholic, though I have worked on this significantly over the years. It can still show up as high standards and expectations of the people around me
What Pushes My Buttons
My Triggers
  • Lack of ownership. When something slips and no one is willing to say that was on me
  • Misalignment or unclear expectations that could have been avoided with a quick conversation
  • Feeling like I am carrying weight that should be shared
  • When people are not being treated fairly or with basic care. I will always notice and it will always bother me
  • High-stakes situations where there is no clarity and no one seems to be steering
  • People acting out of self-interest rather than the greater good. If I sense ego is driving a decision more than what is right for the team or the people we serve, it bothers me
  • People being dishonest or not forthcoming. If I sense someone is skirting the truth or telling me what they think I want to hear, it will erode trust fast
  • Misspelling HIPAA. It stands for Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act. There is no second P. If you work in healthcare and you spell it HIPPA, we will have a conversation
How to Best Work With Me
My User Guide
  • Be direct, clear, and raw with me. I would rather have the honest version than the polished one
  • Take ownership and come with solutions, not just the problem
  • Keep me in the loop, especially when something goes sideways
  • Align with me early on priorities and expectations, not after the fact
  • Push back on my thinking when you disagree. I want the best idea to win, not just mine
  • Give me space to think when I need to go strategic. I don't always do my best thinking in real time
  • Trust that I care about people as much as outcomes. It is not either/or for me
  • If something feels off, say it early. I would always rather deal with it than let it grow
  • Tell me the truth even when it is hard. Honesty and integrity are the foundation of how I want to work with the people around me
